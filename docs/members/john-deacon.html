John Richard Deacon (born 19 August 1951) is an English retired musician, best known as the bass guitarist for Queen. He wrote several of Queen's hits, including "You're My Best Friend", "Another One Bites the Dust", and "I Want to Break Free".

After Freddie Mercury's death, Deacon retired from public life and the music industry, making only a few appearances with Queen before stepping away permanently in 1997.
