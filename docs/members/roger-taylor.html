Roger Meddows Taylor (born 26 July 1949) is an English musician, singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist, best known as the drummer for the rock band Queen. He was voted as one of the greatest drummers in rock music history.

Taylor also wrote or co-wrote many Queen songs, including "Radio Ga Ga", "A Kind of Magic", "These Are the Days of Our Lives", and "Heaven for Everyone". He has also performed with other legendary artists and was involved in side projects like The Cross.
